### listened -- [202602 - listened](https://open.spotify.com/playlist/627qGgdc6CVXPrd121LuQv?si=0a7837ef295a4487)

- [*enema of the garden state*](https://madidiaz.bandcamp.com/album/enema-of-the-garden-state) by madi diaz
    - madi diaz is great. here, she takes seriously what, to my ears, sounds like mark hoppus's attempt to at least consider growing up against tom delong's continued sophomoric antics. for me, this album allows listeners to reconsider the sheen lacquered over the pop punk tunes of yesteryear and delve deeply into songs they've internalized so completely as to forget them as songs at all.
- [*bad moons*](https://americanfootball.bandcamp.com/album/bad-moons) by american football
    - mike kinsella is a bad man. he has been telling us for years. in a recent gq article, he reiterates it once again. bad moons feels different than other 'bad man' kinsella tunes (see [lost](https://owenmusic.bandcamp.com/track/lost-2), [hit and run](https://owenmusic.bandcamp.com/track/hit-and-run), [heir apparent](https://genius.com/American-football-heir-apparent-lyrics), [bad news](https://owenmusic.bandcamp.com/track/bad-news), etc, etc) in that he suggest he might be ready to move beyond recognition. or, at least, he has found someone in that deep darkness to co-haunt. excited for the album
- [*atlas*](https://laurelhalo.bandcamp.com/album/atlas) by laurel halo
    - perfect album. routinely didn't realize it had flipped back to the beginning for 2-3 tracks
